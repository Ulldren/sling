Sling provides a mobile-optimized layout that automatically adapts to smaller screens and touch inputs.

## Layout

* **6 hotbar slots** (Landscape mode) / **3 hotbar slots** (Portrait mode)
* **2 inventory rows** (Landscape mode) / **4 inventory rows** (Portrait mode)

### Interface Navigation

| Action | Control |
| --- | --- |
| **Toggle Inventory** | Tap the **Backpack Icon** in the topbar. |
| **Close Inventory** | Tap the topbar icon again, or tap anywhere on the screen outside of the inventory panel. |

### Touch Gestures

* **Quick Move to Hotbar:** Double-tap any item in your inventory to quickly move it to an empty slot in the hotbar.
* **Drag & Drop:** Drag an item and drop it:
  * **Add/Swap in Hotbar:** Drag from the inventory and drop onto a hotbar slot.
  * **Remove from Hotbar:** Drag from the hotbar and drop outside it to return it to the inventory.
